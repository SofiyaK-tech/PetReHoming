Why we recommend adopting

There are lots of reasons to adopt a pet: you can find a special furry friend, save money, and do a good thing. But let's talk about the facts. Every year, millions of pets end up in shelters, and sadly, many are put to sleep. We're not saying this to make you feel bad, but it's why adoption is so important to us. That's why we hope you'll think about adopting. And since you're here, we bet you're already considering it. It's okay if you choose another way to find a petâ€”we understand everyone's journey is different. We just want to make adopting a pet easier for you however we can.


How to find the perfect pet

Let's clear up a misunderstanding. There's no such thing as a perfect pet. That's because there are many pets out there that could be just right for you. It's all about figuring out what you want. So, start by thinking about what's important to you, like your lifestyle, what kind of pet you like, where you live, and who's in your family. Once you've got that figured out, our team can help you find a pet that matches. Don't forget to sign up for our New Pet Alerts. We'll send you an email when new pets that match what you're looking for become available. That way, you can find your new best friend even faster.
